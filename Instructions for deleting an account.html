Instructions for Deleting an Account

Last updated: December 23, 2024

You can delete your World Words account in the mobile application by following these steps:

  1. Go to Settings (bottom right) (screenshot 1).
  2. Select Profile (screenshot 2).
  3. Click on "Delete Account" (screenshot 3).

Warning! Deleting your World Words account will result in the deletion of all your data and progress. Your account will be permanently removed, including your storage and extensions. Only the cards you created in the world will remain, but you will lose access to them.
